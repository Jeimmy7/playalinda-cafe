About Us

Bringing Caribbean Flavors to Your Events

PlayaLinda Cafe is a family-owned catering business specializing in authentic Puerto Rican cuisine. With recipes passed down through generations, we bring the vibrant flavors and warm hospitality of the Caribbean to your special events.

Our mission is to create memorable dining experiences with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients prepared with traditional techniques. From intimate gatherings to large celebrations, we customize our services to meet your needs.

Whether it's a corporate event, wedding, birthday party, or family reunion, let us transport your guests to the beautiful beaches of Puerto Rico with every bite.
